Funtime Bonnie

Funtime Bonnie is an animatronic rabbit character introduced in Five Nights at Freddy's: Sister Location. He resides on a stage in Circus Baby's Entertainment and Rental, wearing a purple hat with a matching bow tie. Unlike the original Bonnie, he has white fur with faint purple stripes, and his design features a clown-like, more rounded appearance. He is possessed by the soul of a child, contributing to the haunted nature of the facility. In gameplay, he's known for his giggles and erratic movements, often stalking employees, particularly targeting the breaker room. Funtime Bonnie represents the circus-themed horror elements of the game.
