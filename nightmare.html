Nightmare Bonnie

Nightmare Bonnie is a horrifying, nightmarish iteration of the classic Bonnie animatronic from the Five Nights at Freddy's series, originating in *Five Nights at Freddy's 4* (2014) as a manifestation of the protagonist's childhood traumas tied to the haunted pizzeria. In the game's lore, he embodies a twisted, fear-induced projection of the original purple bunny, haunting bedrooms alongside other nightmare figures, with his design possibly drawing from psychological horrors or alternate haunted entities in the franchise's expanded universe. Physically, he sports a tattered, dilapidated purple bunny suit frayed at the edges, revealing glinting endoskeleton bones; oversized, claw-tipped hands; piercing yellow eyes that glow ominously; and a gaping, jagged mouth lined with rows of sharp, metallic teeth, giving him a towering, shadowy, and ethereal monstrous presence that amplifies terror in the darkness.
