Games

Five Nights at Freddy's

Five Nights at Freddy's is a 2014 point-and-click survival horror game developed and published by Scott Cawthon. The player controls Mike Schmidt, a night security guard at a family pizzeria. Schmidt must complete his shifts while avoiding the homicidal animatronic characters that wander the restaurant at night

Five Nights at Freddy's 2

Five Nights at Freddy's 2 is a 2014 point-and-click survival horror game developed and published by Scott Cawthon. The game is a prequel to the original Five Nights at Freddy's, taking place in a new location with new animatronic characters. The player assumes the role of a night security guard who must survive five nights while avoiding the animatronics that roam the building.

Five Nights at Freddy's 3

Five Nights at Freddy's 3 is a 2015 point-and-click survival horror game developed and published by Scott Cawthon. The game is set 30 years after the events of the first game, in a horror attraction based on the original Freddy Fazbear's Pizza. The player takes on the role of a night security guard who must survive five nights while dealing with a single animatronic, Springtrap, as well as hallucinations of other animatronics from the previous games.

Five Nights at Freddy's 4

Five Nights at Freddy's 4 is a 2015 point-and-click survival horror game developed and published by Scott Cawthon. The game is set in the bedroom of a child who is being terrorized by nightmarish versions of the animatronic characters from the previous games. The player must survive five nights while using various mechanics to fend off the animatronics, including closing doors and using a flashlight.

Five Nights at Freddy's World

Five Nights at Freddy's World is a 2016 role-playing game developed and published by Scott Cawthon. The game features characters from the Five Nights at Freddy's series in a colorful, cartoonish world. Players can explore various locations, battle enemies, and complete quests while uncovering the story behind the game's universe.

Five Nights at Freddy's: Sister Location

Five Nights at Freddy's: Sister Location is a 2016 point-and-click survival horror game developed and published by Scott Cawthon. The game takes place in a new location, Circus Baby's Entertainment and Rentals, where the player assumes the role of a technician who must complete various tasks while avoiding the animatronic characters. The game features a more narrative-driven approach compared to previous entries in the series.

Freddy Fazbear's Pizzeria Simulator

Freddy Fazbear's Pizzeria Simulator is a 2017 point-and-click simulation horror game developed and published by Scott Cawthon. The game combines elements of a business simulation game with the traditional survival horror gameplay of the Five Nights at Freddy's series. The player must manage their own pizzeria while also dealing with animatronic characters that become hostile at night.

Ultimate Custom Night

Ultimate Custom Night is a 2018 point-and-click survival horror game developed and published by Scott Cawthon. The game serves as a standalone title in the Five Nights at Freddy's series, allowing players to customize their own night by selecting from a roster of over 50 animatronic characters from previous games. The player must survive against the chosen animatronics while managing various mechanics to stay alive.

Five Nights at Freddy's: Help Wanted

Five Nights at Freddy's: Help Wanted is a 2019 virtual reality survival horror game developed by Steel Wool Studios and published by Scott Cawthon. The game features a collection of mini-games based on previous entries in the Five Nights at Freddy's series, as well as new content. Players can experience the horror of the series in a fully immersive VR environment.

Five Nights at Freddy's: Security Breach

Five Nights at Freddy's: Security Breach is a survival horror game with stealth elements. In the game, the player takes control of a young boy named Gregory, who is locked inside "Freddy Fazbear's Mega Pizzaplex", a large entertainment complex that the player is free to explore. While locked inside, the player must survive from midnight to 6:00 a.m. while fending off several animatronic mascots and robots that attempt to kill them. The complex and animatronics are modeled after a 1980s glam rock style. Gregory will also encounter other types of enemies, such as the night guard Vanessa.

Five Nights at Freddy's: Help Wanted 2

Five Nights at Freddy's: Help Wanted 2, commonly abbreviated to FNaF: HW2, is an independent virtual-reality survival horror video game developed by Steel Wool Studios, Inc., and published by Scottgames, LLC, that was released on December 14, 2023, for SteamVR and PlayStation VR2 and is the tenth installment in the Five Nights at Freddy's series and the second installment in the Help Wanted sub-series of games. A standard non-VR mode was released on June 20, 2024, for Windows and PlayStation 5.

Five Nights at Freddy's: Into the Pit

Five Nights at Freddy's: Into the Pit, commonly abbreviated as FNaF: ItP, is an indie action-adventure horror video game adaptation of the Fazbear Frights story of the same name, Into the Pit. it was developed and published by Mega Cat Studios in partnership with Scottgames and is the seventh spin-off installment of the Five Nights at Freddy's series. The game was initially released on August 7, 2024, on Steam. It was originally going to released on August 8 for the franchise's 10-year anniversary, but following an accidental release on Nintendo Switch, the game was released a day early. This would be followed by PlayStation and Xbox ports on September 27, 2024, and Nintendo Switch on October 31, 2024.

Five Nights at Freddy's: Secret of the Mimic

Five Nights at Freddy's: Secret of the Mimic, commonly abbreviated to FNaF: SotM, is an independent free-roam survival horror video game developed by Steel Wool Studios, Inc. and published by Scottgames, LLC. It was released on June 13, 2025 for Windows and PlayStation 5 and is the eleventh installment in the Five Nights at Freddy's series. A VR version of the game is planned to be released some time in the future.
